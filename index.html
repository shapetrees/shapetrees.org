SHAPE TREES

Github Spec Primer Test Suite

Shape trees marry RDF vocabularies, shapes, and resources into "little trees" that provide machine to machine interoperability, combining them into concepts that humans can easily comprehend, such as medical records, notes, notebooks, calendars, and financial records. For applications that operate on more complex and interconnected resources, Shape Trees express the layout of those resources and associate them with their respective shapes.

Shape Trees are a Solid Community Group work effort.

Changes to the Shape Trees specification and supporting documentation are reviewed by the editors.
